Microwave Self Saucing Chocolate Pudding, serves 1
=================================================

This recipe is for a large serve, or 2x smaller serves.

    Microwave 1.5mins (
        Mix until no lumps (
            Melted (2 1/2tbsp margarine),
            3tbsp SR flour,
            3tbsp sugar,
            1 1/2tbsp cocoa,
            3tbsp milk,
        ),
        Sprinkle on top (
            1tsp cocoa,
            1tsp brown sugar,
        ),
        Gently pour (3tbsp hot water),
    )
